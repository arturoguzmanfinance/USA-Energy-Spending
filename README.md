# USA-Energy-Spending
Objective: Analyze the efficiency and competitive landscape of federal energy contracts. This project investigates how contract pricing types and the volume of competition influence the speed and total cost of project delivery
USA Energy Spending Analysis
Project Objective

This project analyzes energy expenditure trends to understand the financial burden on U.S. households and businesses. In the context of financial services, energy costs are a critical factor in debt-to-income ratios and consumer credit health.

The Data

Volume: 22,000+ rows across two primary datasets.

Focus: Regional price volatility and long-term spending patterns.

Roadmap
